Open a Support Issue

What To Include

  • Your iPhone or iPad model.
  • Your iOS or iPadOS version.
  • The tournament setup, such as number of players and points per match.
  • The steps that led to the issue.
  • A screenshot if it helps explain the problem.

Common Questions

Does the app require internet?
No. Tournament creation, schedule generation, score entry, leaderboard calculation, import, and export are designed to work offline.

Can I share a tournament with another device?
Yes. Export a tournament file and share it with another person who has the app. They can import the file to restore the tournament data.

Does the leaderboard update after score edits?
Yes. Standings are recalculated from the saved match results.
